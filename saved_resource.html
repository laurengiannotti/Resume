About LAUREN

WHY I DO WHAT I LOVE

My passion for innovation and quality has been fueled by my past three years of education at Rochester Institute of Technology in conjunction with the invaluable hands on experience I’ve gained from working with reputable companies within the field. I am currently enrolled in a five year computer engineering program (B.S.) which couples intensive academia with full time engineering work.

My expertise lies in the software industry, where I have an expansive understanding of several programming languages and software development models. I have had the pleasure of sharing my enthusiasm for engineering through a variety of leadership positions as I am the Head of Programming of RIT’s Hot Wheelz Formula SAE Electric Team, a mentor for the RIT computer engineering department’s College and Careers summer program, and a member of the leadership team for the Women in Engineering Open House.
